PROJECTS

In this space you will see some of my past projects I have completed. Models and Collaborators tagged within.

THE WORLDS A LITTLE BLURRY

Digital Design Project. In this project I was required to create a piece of digital content using Adobe photoshop. By using various layers and other techniques I created a billboard style/poster for Billie Eilish's 'The Worlds a Little Blurry' film documentary.

SUKIN SKINCARE

Sukin Skincare, for this Copywriting project I was reqired to produce an advertisement for an Australian brand. I chose to create my own imagery showcasing two of their products and then began to edit them in photoshop too add in the branding and the copy.

DOC MARTINS

In this project I responded to a creative brief that outlined required me to create content based on a pair of shoes. With the help of my good friend Audrey (model pictured), we created posters feating Doc Martin's Cherry Platform Docs. In post production I did some touch ups and then added in the well known Doc Martins logo.

ARCADIA
